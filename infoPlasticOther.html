Recycling Other Plastics

Plastic Codes

No list of recyclable and non-recyclable plastics can be exhaustive, so it helps to look for the code label on packaging. The code can tell you a lot about the recyclability of a plastic.

Codes 1, 2

Plastics with codes 1 and 2 and widely accepted and easily recyclable.
Includes bottles, bags.

Codes 4, 5

Plastics with codes 4 and 5 are still generally accepted but require more specialized machinary.
Includes food containers.

Codes 3, 6, 7

Plastics with codes 3, 6, and 7 are not commonly accepted because they are difficult or not cost-effective to recycle.
Includes styrofoam, toys, CD cases.
