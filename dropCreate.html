Create a Drop Point

Turn recycling into a community effort by creating a Drop Point!

Drop Points let people in your community know that you're collecting certain recyclable items to take to a recycling facility.

Let your neighbors know what you're collecting, and when they can drop off items until, and see the impact of your recycling efforts grow!
