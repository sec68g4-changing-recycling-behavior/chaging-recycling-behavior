Recycling Cardboard

There are multiple ways to recycle your old cardboard. Households can drop cardboard in the recycle bin, while businesses can take a more creative approach.

3 things you can do with cardboard waste:

  1. Cardboard collection: Have a local waste collection company to collect your cardboard waste. Disposing high volumes of cardboard waste can save you a lot on waste disposal costs. You can drop off cardboard waste or schedule a pickup of your cardboard material.
  2. Cardboard shredding: With a cardboard shredder or cardboard perforator you can reuse your cardboard scrap. By shredding or perforating cardboard boxes and sheets of cardboard you can make your own packaging material.
  3. Cardboard baling: With a cardboard baler you compress your cardboard waste into a compact bale. Cardboard is very bulky, so these machines really clear up space in your company. You can market the cardboard bales to a recycling company in your area and make revenue from your cardboard waste.
