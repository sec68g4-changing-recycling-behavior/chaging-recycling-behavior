Recycling Plastic Bags

Preparation

Most, if not all plastic bags are able to be recycled. Just make sure there is nothing left inside your bags before dropping them off. Frequent offenders include stickers, receipts, and food crumbs.

Did You Know?

Plastic bags are among the most common sources of marine debris, endagering fish, seabirds, and aquatic vegetation. By recycling bags, you are saving lives!
