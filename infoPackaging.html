Recycling Plastic Packaging

Before You Start

Make sure the product packaging you have is indeed recyclable! A number of common household goods do not come in easily recyclable packaging, including potato chips, yogurt, and toothpaste. They either contain too many layers (chip bags), use different forms of plastic (yogurt cups), or contaminate easily (toothpaste tubes). Do research!

Common Recyclable Packaging

  • water carton film
  • cereal bag
  • bubble wrap
  • book or paper packaging
