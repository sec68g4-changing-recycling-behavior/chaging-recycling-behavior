Recycling Plastic Bottles

Preparation

While almost all plastic bottles and jugs are recyclable, it helps to rinse them out beforehand! Doing so will your bottles easier for recycling centers to process.

Things to Look Out For

In some cases, especially if it has been used to hold chemicals or other hazardous waste, a plastic bottle cannot be recycled. When in doubt, throw the bottle away to prevent it from contaminating other, safe bottles.
